About the Study

Spatial-HRTF Comparative Audio Analysis

This research explores how different audio rendering techniques affect user engagement and immersion in virtual cultural heritage environments.

Using cutting-edge HRTF (Head-Related Transfer Function) technology, we're comparing traditional stereo audio with spatial audio in a 360° virtual tour of Saratha Vilas, a beautifully restored Chettinad mansion in Tamil Nadu.

Your participation will help us understand how audio technologies can enhance cultural preservation and create more meaningful virtual experiences.
